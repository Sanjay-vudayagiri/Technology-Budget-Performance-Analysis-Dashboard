**1. Project Title**

Technology Budget & Performance Analysis Dashboard | Power BI Business Intelligence Project

**2.Purpose**

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze technology budgets, forecast performance, and financial variance across business areas, IT functions, cost elements, and countries.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor Plan vs Actual vs Forecast performance and make data-driven financial decisions.

**5. Features** 

**5.1 Business Problem**

Organizations face challenges in tracking technology spending effectively.
There is often limited visibility into:

Budget utilization across departments

Variance between Plan, Forecast, and Actual spending

Cost distribution across regions

Overspending or underperforming business units

This reduces financial control and delays strategic decisions.

**5.2 Goal of the Dashboard**

Provide a centralized view of financial performance

Compare Plan, Forecast, and Actual values

Identify positive and negative variances

Analyze spending across Business Areas, IT Areas, Countries, Cost Elements, and Months

Enable interactive filtering and drill-down analysis

**5.3 Walkthrough of Key Visuals** 

KPI Cards
Displays Total Actual, Total Forecast, Plan vs Actual Variance, Plan vs Forecast Variance, and Variance Percentage.
Provides a quick executive-level summary.

Monthly Trend Analysis (Stacked Column Chart)
Compares Plan, Forecast, and Actual values across months to identify trends and deviations.

Country-wise Variance Table
Shows Plan vs Actual variance by country to highlight top-performing and underperforming regions.

Cost Element Donut Chart
Displays distribution of spending across different cost elements to identify major cost drivers.

IT Area and Business Area Bar Charts
Compares financial performance across departments to detect budget deviations.

Slicers
Business Area, IT Area, and Month filters enable dynamic and interactive analysis.

**5.4 Business Impact & Insights**

Improved financial visibility across technology departments

Faster identification of budget variances

Better resource allocation decisions

Clear identification of high-cost regions and departments

Supports proactive financial management
